== Description ==

Embed Picasa Album

Display your Picasa albums to your pages and articles.
Use the Embed tag available in your account google picasa.
Very easy to use, identify yourself and insert ...
Automatic update of the list of albums. 
Log in once or when necessary, do not remember your password.
